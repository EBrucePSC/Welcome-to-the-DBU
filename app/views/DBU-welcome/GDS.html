Who we are

In 2021 The PSC split into 3 business units one of which was the DBU. We help public sector clients with an array of digital needs, helping them adapt to a rapidly changing digital landscape. Currently any consultant might be drafted onto a DBU project, or asked to help us win bids, but we have a few permanent managers and partners.

Meet the team

Phil Buckley, Digital Director

A digital specialist who has won multiple awards and led many successful transformations across Government, Phil has been with The PSC since 2019 and is our first ever Digital Director. Phil’s drive, optimism, and unflappability have helped our clients achieve major savings and improvements in user satisfaction, while resolving some of their most complex and intractable problems.

Dr Antonio Weiss, Senior Partner

Antonio specialises in using data science and new technologies to design effective public services, with a specific focus on education, administrative and health and care systems. Antonio believes that the strength, resilience and performance of public services are key to successful, diverse and high-functioning societies.

Katie Burns, Senior Manager

Katie is known at The PSC for her creativity and love of primary research. She previously worked in Insight and as an Innovation Manager for a leading UK children’s charity, and is now a dedicated member of The PSC’s digital unit.

Tushar Mehta, Product Manager

Compassionate and trustworthy, Tushar believes in open and transparent communication at all levels. With a broad range of experience, he brings a unique perspective to problem solving with a strong and clear focus on final goals.

Mark Buckley, Senior Product Manager

Mark has spent the last 10 years working in digital and honed his skills in product management at Government Digital Service. Using practical experience, he keeps the user front of mind while understanding how that fits in with technology choices and organisational capability.
