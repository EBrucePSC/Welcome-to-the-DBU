Bids

As a Consultant or Associate Consultant, your first involvement with the DBU might be to help us win a bid for work. If we are successful, there is a good chance you will get to work on that project. We bid for work in two major ways depending on what kind of work we are trying to win:

Client referrals

These are often referred to as 'warm tenders' meaning someone we already have a working relationship with has encouraged us to bid for work. We might even have helped to right the tender process. This type of bid tends to apply to strategy, investment, and audit work.

It usually takes around six weeks from submitting a bid to knowing if we have won, but the process can take months overall.

Digital Marketplace

This is a website where we make 'cold' bids, normally for digital services and managment work. Bids here involve writing 100 word answers to a set of questions. We tend to have a hit rate of 5-10% on these.
