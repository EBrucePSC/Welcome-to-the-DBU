Strategy

Strategy is the type of work most familiar to regular consultants. It involves user research and problem solving to work out exactly what the problem is, before coming up with a solution.

The key skills you need for a strategy project are unviersal to consulting. Skills like stakeholder influencing, problem solving and project managment. The only difference is that, on a digital project, you have to have some knowledge of the landscape. Such as; how services work, how they are built and maintained, and how much all of this costs.
