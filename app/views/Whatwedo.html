What we do

We work with our clients, using data science and user centred-design to co-create new digital services, ensuring they deliver strong financial results, are in harmony with teams and working practices, and include everyone.

Whilst clients can approach the DBU at any stage of a project it can be helpful to think of our work as a journey we help clients navigate along. The journey might start with a us submitting a bid for a project the client needs help with such as developing a digital strategy, and then continue all the way through designing or purchasing a digital service or conducting an audit for what they already have.

At every stage we stop and ask ourselves; "is the PSC the right organisation to deliver the best results for this work?"
