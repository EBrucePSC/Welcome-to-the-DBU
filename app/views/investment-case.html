Investment case

An investment case helps to persuade officals of the need to spend money on a particular service. It involves translating a knowledge of digital services into language that is appealing to financial decision makers, such as treasury officials.

One investment case we worked on was called 'Government as a platform.' This focussed on transforming the digital offering onf the UK government.

One upshot of this was the decision to develop the toolkit that built this website
